PVC Couch

October 2014 to December 2014

Oh, freshman year. I think this projected reflected my roommate and my priorities for the year. We need a place to chill on, a surface for beer pong, and a spare bed for visitors. This couch was more than just a couch; it was all those things. With an extra set of legs, and a detachable back panel, it truly was a transformer.

We even got a small scale project grant from the engineering student government. I'm always proud when I get to do something cool for free!

The ingenuity of the design was ultimately it's downfall. One day in April, with far too many people on it, the back panel joints broke :'(
